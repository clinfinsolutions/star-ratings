— Data Sources & Methodology —
CMS Overall Hospital Star Rating
Sourced from the Hospital General Information dataset on the CMS Provider Data Catalog. 2025 Release was published April 30, 2025 and reflects measure performance windows that vary by individual measure (typically 12–36 months ending 6 months prior to release). The 2026 Release is scheduled for May 13, 2026; this tool will display the comparison once the release is live.
CMS HAC Reduction Program
Sourced from CMS HAC Reduction Program Hospital files. The Total HAC Score is calculated from PSI 90 (Patient Safety Indicator composite) and five hospital-acquired infection measures (CLABSI, CAUTI, SSI, CDI, MRSA). Hospitals in the worst-performing quartile receive a 1% Medicare payment reduction.

FY2025 measurement windows: PSI 90 covers Jul 2021–Jun 2023; HAI measures cover Jan 2022–Dec 2023. FY2026 measurement windows: PSI 90 covers Jul 2022–Jun 2024; HAI measures cover Jan 2023–Dec 2024.
A note on correlation
Star Rating's Safety of Care measure group and the HAC Reduction Program use overlapping but not identical measure sets and statistical methods. The two penalties are highly correlated but not equivalent: HAC penalty status is a strong predictor of Safety of Care quartile placement, but exceptions exist. Both are influenced by the underlying documentation, coding, and POA designation accuracy of the same set of cases.
